How to book a shipment with

JoTDSM International Logistics?

Step 1

Booking & Order Entry

Client books a service. Shipment details (origin, destination, volume, mode) are logged into the system.

Step 2

Scheduling & Allocation

Shipment is assigned to the optimal vehicle or partner route using load matching & route planning tools.

Step 3

Pickup & Origin Scanning

The consignment is picked up and scanned at the origin point, generating the docket and shipment ID.

Step 4

Transit & Tracking

Cargo moves through the network (direct or hub-based). Real-time tracking updates are shared with clients.

Step 5

Delivery at Destination

Delivery is made as per appointment or SLA; POD is collected (digital or manual) and updated in the system.

Step 6

Documentation & Closure

Invoices, LRs, and PODs are compiled. Client receives final delivery confirmation and billing summary.

Step 7

Feedback & Support

Post-delivery feedback is collected. Any issue resolution or escalation is managed by the support team.
